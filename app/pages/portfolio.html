Quikpoll

  • A polling platform that sends an SMS poll out to a group of users, quickly receives feedback, and immediately displays the results.
  • Currently being used internally at select companies.
  • Built with Express, Node.js, MongoDB, Angular, Twilio, Chart.js, Firebase and Webpack.
    • [website][github]

Waste Not

  • AngelHack Seattle 2016 Grand Prize Winner.
  • A platform that addresses consumer waste by connecting businesses to social and community organizations:- businesses can inventory items that they plan on throwing out and social and community organisations can claim these items for redistribution.
  • Built in 24 hours for AngelHack Seattle 2016 using Express, Node.js, MongoDB, React, Esri and Webpack.
    • [heroku beta][github]

Alfred - The Smart Mirror

  • An IoT smart mirror built with JavaScript and Raspberry Pi:- a customisable client-side dashboard which pushes directly to the mirror display.
  • Built in four days using Electron, Express, Node.js, jQuery, Angular, MongoDB, Socket.IO, Sass, Gulp, Mocha, Chai and Webpack.
    • [heroku][github]

Saphero

  • A client-side dashboard that easily connects and controls Orbotix devices; 400+ npm downloads in the first month.
  • Built in four days using Sphero API, Node.js, Socket.IO, Express, Jade, Sass, Gulp, Mocha, Chai, Noble and Serialport.
    • [npm][github]

House of Gift Cards
