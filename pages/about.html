Hello there! My name is Sabrina and I am originally from Malaysia but went to boarding schools in England and Australia. I got a B.A. in International Affairs from Northeastern University in Boston and as part of my degree, I did two six-month long internships: the first at a museum in Hawaii and the second at a start-up in New York City. Upon graduation, I moved to Indonesia and worked at a non-profit foundation that empowers women through skills education.

It was when I began designing a new Wordpress website for the foundation that I began playing around with basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I was intrigued and began delving into Codecademy courses and free online tutorials. Soon, I was enrolled in Code Fellows' advanced course in full-stack JavaScript - a place where I could immerse myself full-time in an intensive and collaborative working environment to further my programming skills.

I really enjoy solving problems, seeing the end to end growth of a product, and working as a team to deliver an innovative solution. I've also found a passion in creating social change through innovative business and technological means.

Today, I'm still building my programming skills but as a junior software developer at Red Ape Solutions, a tech consultancy based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
